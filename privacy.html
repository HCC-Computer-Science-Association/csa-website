Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 12, 2025

Introduction

The Computer Science Association (CSA) at Houston City College is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This policy explains how we handle information when you interact with our organization and website.

Information We Don't Collect

CSA does not collect, store, or process any personal data through our website. Our website is purely informational and does not require user registration or data collection.

Third-Party Services

Our website may link to external services (such as Discord, Eagle Engage, or social media platforms). These services have their own privacy policies, and CSA is not responsible for their data collection practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Website Security

Technical Measures

  • Encryption: All website traffic uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Secure Hosting: Website hosted on secure, regularly updated servers
  • Regular Updates: Website security is maintained through regular updates

Third-Party Services

Required Services

  • GitHub Pages: Hosting our static website (GitHub privacy policy applies)
  • Email Service: Email communication through your preferred email provider
  • Discord: Member communication platform (Discord privacy policy applies)

Optional Services

  • Social Media: Links to our social media accounts (respective privacy policies apply)
  • External Links: Links to external resources and partners

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. When we make significant changes:

  • We'll notify members via email
  • We'll post a notice on our website
  • We'll update the "Last updated" date
  • We'll maintain previous versions for reference

Acknowledgment

By joining CSA or using our website, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this privacy policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not join CSA or use our services.
